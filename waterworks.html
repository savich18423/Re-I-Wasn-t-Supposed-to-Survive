🧃 Build Your Own Bottled Water Empire

💼 Business Sim  ·  🛠 Early Access  ·  🎮 PC

Start your journey in a small garage and grow into a full-fledged water bottling company!
Order empty bottles, fill them by hand, pack, and deliver to customers. Expand your workshop, hire staff, and automate your factory — from startup to water mogul.

“Every drop counts. Every bottle is yours.”
🔗 View on Steam

🎮 Gameplay Loop

  • 📥 Order empty bottles via your in-game computer
  • 🚛 Unpack deliveries and prep your station
  • 💧 Fill bottles manually using the pump
  • 🧤 Close, pack, and box your product
  • 📦 Ship to clients and earn profit
  • 💵 Reinvent and expand your factory space
  • 🛠 Automate — hire workers, improve tools

⭐ Features

  • Manual, step-by-step production mechanics
  • Minimal 3D style with third-person camera
  • Customizable workshop: floor, walls, furniture
  • Employee system: hire & assign tasks
  • Factory upgrades & automation options
  • Business growth with engaging economy

📅 Early Access Plans

The game will launch in Early Access on Steam. We plan to regularly update it based on community feedback — adding more mechanics, gear, customization and economic features over time.
